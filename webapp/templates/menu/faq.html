What's the point of the app?

The app is designed to practice your German grammar and conjugation through isolated exercises.
Each exercise aims at practicing one particular competency such as A2 connectors, B1 prepositions, Partizip II etc.
Through this isolated practice, you'll be able to apply these competencies with more ease while writing and speaking.
The app is NOT designed to replace your current course (teacher, books, apps etc.).

How to best use the app?

You should be following a course with a teacher, with a book or with another app and aiming at a particular level (A1, A2 etc.).
Practice the material you've already discovered by doing the exercises that fit your level.
Do not practice too many exercises at the same time; practice up to 3 exercises and 30 minutes a day regularly.
Feel free to use external help while doing the exercises such as declension tables and dictionaries.

Do you provide any learning material?

No I don't. Plenty of learning material already exist online and offline, made by people far more experienced and knowledgeable than me.

What do the "XX%" mean?

Percentages show your score for a particular level. Scores are then aggregated for each exercise.
Below 60%, I highly recommend you read again the theory.
Between 60% and 85%, practice again after a good night's sleep.
Above 85%, you have a good grasp of the material.

Can I do an exercise and level multiple times?

Yes. Once you finish a level, you can click on the Refresh button to reinitialize it. It will also reinitialize the score.
You can also refresh a level before finishing it by clicking on the white circular arrow (↺) next to the progress inside the level.

I think I've noticed an error in an exercise.

Send me an email with a screenshot and an explanation of the error. If there's indeed an error I'll correct it for the next update.

Where is my progress stored?

Progress is stored on pythonanywhere's servers.

Why has my progress been erased?

Your progress has likely been saved, but its retrieval is based on a session ID saved by your browser in a cookie. If you use a VPN, or if you use a browser that has protection against cookies, your progress cannot be retrieved.

I use this app on multiple devices (phone, computer, tablet etc.). Is there a way to share the progress on multiple devices?

Unfortunately not. The progress is stored individually for each device.
I do not wish to create logins and accounts for users as I do not wish to store email addresses and other personal data.
